1. Find what you want to buy

Search through top brands and amazing deals available from US stores like Amazon, Ebay and many more to your heart’s content and select your desired products. We can offer you an exclusive pricing for products, ensuring all your purchases are tax free.

2. Get quoted!

Simply send us a link to your product and fill our request form along with your product options such as quantity, colour etc. We’ll check availability and contact you at your convenience and send you a quotation of the total price along with the shipping and processing fees. In addition to that, you have the option to combine packages as well. Our pricing is based on the weight of the shipment as well as how you would be purchasing and opting to ship your product to your doorstep. There are multiple options available for you to choose from. Check out our pricing page (insert drop down shit)

Pricing

A processing fee of 10$ will be charged for every order. The price of every product depends on the total weight (in pounds) of the package. For every pound we charge a fee of 15$.

3. Payment and Shipping

Once you receive the quotation and provided that you’re satisfied with the total pricing, you’ll be required to make the payment via BML online transfer, credit/debit card transaction or cash pickup. Once the payment is complete, we’ll make the order and contact you with the confirmation. Keep in mind, the time of your product arrival to our US warehouse varies depending on the vendors (i.e: Amazon and Ebay). Once it reaches us from the US, it’ll take 1-4 days to ship from our US warehouse to the Maldives and we’ll get it to your doorstep in 1-2 days. Please consider, this may vary due to unforeseen circumstances and we apologize in advance for any inconvenience.

4. Product Tracking and Customer Service

As your package begins its journey to you from overseas, our team will be working around the clock to make sure that you’ll receive timely updates on its progress. We ensure to uphold our delivery standards by getting your packages to you within the designated time frame as mentioned above and guarantee that your product is in tip-top shape.
